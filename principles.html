Principles

Design principles

The six rules every Pennymac design decision defers to.

01
Calm over clever
Mortgage is a high-stakes, regulated product. Favor legibility, generous spacing, and plain language over flashy motion.
02
Name the thing
Every field has a visible label. Placeholder text is not a label. Every dollar figure is tappable for breakdown.
03
Show your work
Rates, fees, and calculations always show their inputs. Surprise is the enemy of trust.
04
Forgiving by default
Validate on blur, not keystroke. Destructive actions are always two-step. Recoverable by design.
05
Touch-first
44px minimum hit target on mobile, 32px on desktop. Thumb-reachable primary actions.
06
Accessible non-negotiable
AA contrast everywhere. Color is never the only signal. Focus rings always visible.
