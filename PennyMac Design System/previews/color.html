Usage rules

Do
Use blue-700 for primary CTAs and key brand surfaces. Deeper blue-900 for high-contrast dark headers.
Accent
Gold is accent-only: highlights, small graphic marks, and the logo triangle. Never a button background.
Don't
Pair semantic color without an icon or label — color alone fails accessibility for 1 in 12 users.
